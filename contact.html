"Louis, I think this is the beginning of a beautiful friendship." Casablanca

Call us old-fashioned for our love of black-and-white movies, analog over digital, and a preference to see our friends face-to-face. We've got a Facebook page and a Twitter account; but we feel updates and tweets are better left to Tom Brokaw and the birds.

The best way to experience The Enchanted Rock Garden is to walk through our door (preferably during our open hours). Hopefully some time spent meandering through the minerals, metaphysical, jewelry, books, gifts and home decor, you'll discover some finds of your own as well as our truth...

We may be the twin cities' largest rock shop, but we're a gem unto ourselves.
