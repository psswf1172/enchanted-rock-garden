Info

Our goal is to have something for any type of collector. Our customers include children, as well as top mineral collectors and museums. In short, we are crazy about rocks and it shows.

We carry beginner-advanced collector pieces, metaphysical stones, rare species, decor pieces, lapidary stones, tumbled stones, fluorescents, lake superior agates, fossils, jewelry, rock-related gifts, beads, books and much more.
