About

Fine minerals and the unusual.

The Enchanted Rock Garden is a rock shop in Richfield, Minnesota, a near-suburb of Minneapolis. We have been in business since 1992.The shop is owned by Peter Giangrande and managed by Mary John.

Our shop specializes in diversity. Our goal is to have something for any type of collector. We carry beginner to advanced collector pieces, metaphysical stones, rare species, decor pieces, lapidary stones, tumbled stones, fluorescents, lake superior agates, fossils, jewelry, rock-related gifts, beads, books and much more. Our customers include children, as well as top mineral collectors and museums. In short, we are crazy about rocks and it shows.
