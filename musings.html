  • My Mother's Birthday Sale

    2016 Oct 16 -- A long, long time ago in a location not so far away, my uncle opened his shop on the most fortuitous of days, my mother's birthday.

    I'd love to get out there for the sale, but it's Apple Fest weekend. - JO ANN ABELLA

    This doesn't mean you should miss it. Shop like Carrie Fisher, and buy some rocks from my uncle.

    Or don't and a pox be upon you.

  • East Coast Rock Show

    2016 Aug 7 -- 200 dealers from all over the U.S. and abroad, offering a variety of items related to earth sciences. Door prizes, guest speakers, museum quality exhibits, and a large wholesale section. Air-conditioned hall. 2016 Exhibitor is Peter Megaw: Minerales Mexicanos – The Peter Megaw Collection

    If it weren't for the rocks in it's bed, the stream would have no song. - CARL PERKINS

    The exhibitors may be from all over the world, but some of the items on display are literally from out of this world. Meteorites are just some of the rocks you can view at the 2015 East Coast Gem, Mineral and Fossil show running through Sunday on the grounds of the Eastern States Exposition in West Springfield. With over 200 exhibitors, show officials plan on about 9,000 people coming through the gate over three days.

  • A Tiny Wonder

    2015 July 7 -- Two summers ago my uncle nearly lost his mind over a purchase no bigger than a pencil eraser. For real, it was tiny and suuuuuuper expensive. And he nearly forgot it at my sister's house

    If my uncle sang songs of rocks, he'd have sung one about this one. - JEN ABELLA

    He had reason to be thrilled though. It was maybe the third, or perhaps even second, largest specimen of it's kind. Harvard would have come knocking for it, but they had the only one bigger.

    So now it is safely stored with my uncle's collection, admired by it's shelf-mates but otherwise a rock.
